HISTORY OF INNOVATION

~   AT&T AdWorks Lab   ~

Launch Site
The AT&T AdWorks Lab tasked my team with creating an interactive experience to showcase AT&T Lab's rich "History of Innovation". The experience was built for the web and was translated to touch via some interactive display adapters on the TVs. Using a combination of Design, UX/UI, Motion Graphics, and Web Development we created a rich experience that works across Web, Mobile and Tablet. Special thanks to Hungry for their technical guidance and top notch development work and Jenny Goldstick for her help establishing the initial concept.
