A LOOK INTO THE FUTURE

~   AT&T AdWorks Lab   ~

Our team was tasked with demonstrating a "Look Into the Future of Advertising" through the eyes of a Media Buyer. The goal was blend real world scenarios happening in realtime with a Media Buyer who is strategically adjusting their campaign to reach their customers at the right time, right place and with the right message.

 

Aesthetically, we wanted this project to feel "futuristic, clean, sleek, modern". Some of our inspiration can be found below. 

 

We had an amazing team that we assembled for this project, a combination of in-house and out of house production.
